# Answer Engine Content Template (GEO Optimized)

Use this template for the **Introduction** section of all new blog posts. The goal is to maximize visibility in AI search results (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) by providing direct, data-rich answers immediately.

---

## 1. Title
**Format**: `[Main Keyword]: [Constraint/Specific Value] Guide`
*Example*: "Best Time to Visit Mongolia: Weather & Festival Guide 2025"

## 2. The Direct Answer (BLUF - Bottom Line Up Front)
**Instruction**: The VERY FIRST paragraph must answer the user's intent directly. Do not "warm up" with fluff. Provide specific data points (dates, costs, numbers).

**Template**:
> "[Direct Answer]. specifically [Condition A] and [Condition B]. Key factors include [Data Point 1] and [Data Point 2]."

**Example**:
> "The best time to visit Mongolia is **June to August**, when temperatures range from 20°C to 25°C (68°F–77°F). For the Eagle Festival, visit in **early October**. Avoid November through March unless you are prepared for -30°C winters."

## 3. Key Takeaways Table (Snapshot)
**Instruction**: A markdown table or list summarizing the critical facts. AIs love extracting this.

| Factor | Recommendation | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Peak Season** | June - August | Best weather, Naadam Festival |
| **Shoulder Season** | May, September | Fewer crowds, cooler nights |
| **Budget Friendly** | October - April | Flights are ~30% cheaper |
| **Visa Requirement** | 34 Countries Visa-Free | Includes South Korea, Most EU |

## 4. Entity-Rich Context
**Instruction**: In the next 2-3 paragraphs, mention related entities to build topical authority.
*   **Entities to weave in**: "Gobi Desert," "Ulaanbaatar," "Ger Camps," "Altai Mountains."

**Example**:
> "While most travelers land in **Ulaanbaatar**, the real adventure begins in the **Gobi Desert** to the south or the **Altai Mountains** in the west. When planning your **Mongolia itinerary**, factor in at least 2 days for travel between regions due to vast distances."
